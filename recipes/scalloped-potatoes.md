## Ingredients
- Potatoes
- Heavy cream or milk
- Vegeta or Chicken stock
- Grated cheese of your choice

## Method

1. Peel potatoes roughly sliced
2. Layer on a tray, next to each other, sitting up right
3. Set stove top to high heat
4. Put on the tray on the stove top
5. Add Water, stock/vegeta on top of the potatoes (add nutmeg if Basim is not watching)
6. Get it to boil
7. Once water reduces by half, turn off
8. Put in tray milk/heavy cream, cheese
9. Add the tray to oven at 200 degrees
10. Cook for about 38 minutes
