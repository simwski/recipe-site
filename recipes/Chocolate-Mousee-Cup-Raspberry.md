## Background
I (Simone) first witnessed Basim make this during AFL Grand final day of 2023. We were a few months into dating and he was hosting an AFL watch party with his friends and family in Hawthorn. It was the first time we prepared together. The timing in the oven for this meal is everything: any time over and you risk it all. 


Prep was going well, choccie mousse were in the oven and Basim went to shower. I was tasked with tidying up and watching the mousse. Suddenly, the oven beeped at me. I matched its energy and stared back at it. For context, I was weary of this oven, as it was a smart oven that I was lowkey high key intimidated by. I then chose to ignored it and return to tidying. 


(Very) shorty afterwards, Basim emerged from the shower. Osama arrived to deliver the esky. Basim, noticing the mousse, hurriedly pulled them out: They had been in just the tiniest bit too long. I mentioned the oven did beep at me (but I left it). Basim turned to me and yelled,
 “YOU’VE RUINED EVERYTHING”.
