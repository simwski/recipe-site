## Background
We had a very indulgent weekend so I was craving for something light and comforting. Prior to this cook, I had never had tomato soup. Now I am converted.

Add protein to this meal with a side of a tuna cheese toastie.

## Ingredients
- Variety of different tomatoes (roma and gourmet is what I used)
- Dried basil
- Smoked paprika
- 1l chicken stock
- 5 garlic cloves
- Diced carrot
- Fresh parsley stem, chopped finely
- 1 onion

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees celcius.
2. Cut the tomatoes in half. Make sure to cut a “V” out of their tops to remove the core.
3. Marinate the tomatoes in olive oil, salt, pepper, smoked paprika and basil. Don't be shy at this stage, the more the better.
4. Line the tray with baking paper, and lay the tomatoes face up on a tray.
5. Place about 5 garlic cloves (with the skin still on) amongst the tomatoes.
6. Roast the tomatoes for about 40-45 minutes.
7. Get a big pot and saute onion, carrot and parsley stem.
8. Put the garlic cloves aside. Place the tomatoes into the pot with all their juices.
9. Squeeze the garlic cloves out of their flesh and into the pot.
10. Add the chicken stock.
11. Blitz all ingredients until soup is smooth.
12. Let simmer for about 10 mins.
